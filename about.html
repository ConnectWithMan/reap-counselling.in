Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the complex journey of engineering admissions in Rajasthan. We are dedicated to providing students with clear, accurate, and personalized guidance for the REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process). We believe every student deserves a fair chance to secure a spot in their dream college, and we strive to empower them with the knowledge and support needed to navigate the counseling process with confidence.

Meet Our Counsellor

Manish Meena

With years of experience in the educational sector, Manish Meena has established himself as a trusted independent admission counsellor. His deep understanding of the REAP process, combined with a genuine passion for helping students, has enabled hundreds of aspiring engineers to find their path. Manish is committed to providing ethical, transparent, and effective counselling to ensure students and their families feel supported every step of the way.

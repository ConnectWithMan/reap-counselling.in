Participating Government & Private Institutes in Rajasthan

Participating Institutes in REAP 2026

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) provides a unified platform for admissions into various reputed Government-aided and Private unaided engineering colleges across Rajasthan. Candidates can choose their preferred institutions during the Choice Filling stage based on their merit rank.

Below is a categorized list of some of the top participating engineering colleges under REAP 2026. Please note that this list highlights prominent institutions, and there are many other affiliated colleges available during the counseling process.

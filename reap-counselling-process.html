REAP 2026 Counselling Overview

The REAP Counselling Process is a centralized, fully online procedure conducted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan. It facilitates admission to undergraduate engineering (B.E./B.Tech) and architecture (B.Arch) programs across government and private colleges in Rajasthan.

The process generally involves registration, fee payment, document upload, choice filling, seat allotment, and final reporting. Admissions are based on JEE Main Rank, Class 12th Percentile, or Diploma Percentage.

Step-by-Step Counselling Procedure

Step 1: Online Registration & Fee Payment

Candidates must visit the official REAP website and register using their basic details. A registration fee (approx. Rs. 295/-) must be paid online. This is a non-refundable fee.

Step 2: Document Upload

After payment, upload scanned copies of necessary documents (photo, signature, mark sheets, category certificate) in the prescribed format (JPG/JPEG).

Step 3: Choice Filling & Locking

This is the most critical step. Candidates must select their preferred colleges and branches. Choices should be arranged in decreasing order of preference. Once satisfied, Lock the choices. If not locked manually, they will auto-lock on the last date.

Step 4: Seat Allotment

CEG releases the allotment result based on merit and choices. Candidates can check their status on the portal. If allotted, a provisional allotment letter is generated.

Step 5: Reporting to Institute

Candidates must physically report to the allotted college within the stipulated time with original documents and the remaining admission fee to confirm their seat.

Documents Required for Counselling

Candidates must carry the following original documents along with two sets of self-attested photocopies at the time of reporting. For a detailed list, check Documents Required for REAP.

  • Printout of Provisional Seat Allotment Letter.
  • REAP 2026 Application Form & Fee Receipt.
  • Aadhar Card / Valid Photo ID.
  • Class 10th Marksheet (Proof of Date of Birth).
  • Class 12th Marksheet / Diploma Certificate.
  • JEE Main 2026 Score Card (if applicable).
  • Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan candidates).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/EWS) if applicable.
  • Medical Fitness Certificate.
  • Income Certificate (for TFWS candidates).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the REAP 2026 Counselling Fee?

The registration fee is typically around Rs. 295/-. It is non-refundable and must be paid online.

2. How many rounds are there in REAP Counselling?

There are usually 3 to 4 rounds, including Upward Movement and Spot Rounds for vacant seats.

3. Can I change my college choices after locking?

No, once choices are locked, they cannot be modified. If you don't lock them manually, they auto-lock on the deadline.

4. What is Upward Movement?

Upward Movement allows candidates to upgrade their allotted seat to a higher preference in their choice list if seats become available in subsequent rounds.

5. Is physical reporting necessary?

Yes, candidates must physically report to the allotted institute with original documents to confirm their admission.

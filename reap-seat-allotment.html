REAP Seat Allotment 2026

Check Result & Download Letter

Allotment Overview

The REAP 2026 Seat Allotment result declares which college and branch has been assigned to you based on your General Merit Rank, Category, and the Priority of Options you submitted. Candidates who are allotted a seat must download their Provisional Allotment Letter and report to the allotted institute within the scheduled time to confirm their admission.

How to Check Result

Follow these steps to check your allotment status:

  • Step 1: Login - Visit the official REAP website and log in to the Candidate Panel.
  • Step 2: Status - Click on the "Check Seat Allotment Status" link.
  • Step 3: View Details - Your allotted college and branch will be displayed on the screen.
  • Step 4: Download Letter - If you are satisfied or want to participate in upward movement, download the Provisional Allotment Letter.
  • Step 5: Print - Take multiple printouts of the letter as it is required at the time of reporting.

Documents for Reporting

Candidates must carry the following original documents and self-attested copies when reporting to the allotted institute:

  • Provisional Allotment Letter.
  • REAP 2026 Application Form & Challan Copy.
  • 10th and 12th Class Marksheets.
  • Domicile Certificate (if applicable).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/MBC) if applicable.
  • Income Certificate (for TFWS candidates).
  • Medical Fitness Certificate.
  • Aadhar Card.
Upward Movement Process

If you are not satisfied with the allotted college/branch, you can apply for Upward Movement. To do this, you must first report to the currently allotted institute and submit your documents/fee. Then, apply online for Upward Movement. If a seat in a higher preference becomes available, you will be shifted; otherwise, your current seat remains safe.
